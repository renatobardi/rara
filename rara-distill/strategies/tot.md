# STRATEGY — Tree of Thought

Before producing the final JSON object, explore a few angles internally and keep the
best:

1. Consider 2-3 different framings of what the source is really about.
2. For each framing, sketch which concepts/insights it would surface.
3. Pick the framing that best fits the source and is most useful for later retrieval,
   then merge in any strong items the other framings surfaced.
4. Assemble the final answer from the chosen framing.

Do your exploration silently. The response must still be ONLY the final JSON object
described below — do not include your intermediate reasoning in the output.
